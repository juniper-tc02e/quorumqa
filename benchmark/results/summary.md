# QuorumQA Benchmark Results

- Questions: 90
- Baseline (single flagship-tier call) accuracy: 84.4%, $0.02396/question
- QuorumQA accuracy: 78.9%, $0.02132/question
- Self-consistency@5 (stretch baseline) accuracy: 58.9%, $0.00930/question
- Escalation rate (questions needing Skeptic/Verifier/Judge): 37.8%
- False-escalation rate (Judge invoked but only re-confirmed the plurality): 58.8%
- Of 14 plurality overturns by the Judge, 11 were correct (78.6%)
- QuorumQA cost delta vs baseline: -0.00264 USD/question (-11.0%)
