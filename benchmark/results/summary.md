# QuorumQA Benchmark Results

- Questions: 89
- Baseline (single flagship-tier call) accuracy: 84.3%, $0.02407/question
- QuorumQA accuracy: 78.7%, $0.02135/question
- Self-consistency@5 (stretch baseline) accuracy: 59.6%, $0.00936/question
- Escalation rate (questions needing Skeptic/Verifier/Judge): 37.1%
- False-escalation rate (Judge invoked but only re-confirmed the plurality): 60.6%
- Of 13 plurality overturns by the Judge, 10 were correct (76.9%)
- QuorumQA cost delta vs baseline: -0.00272 USD/question (-11.3%)
