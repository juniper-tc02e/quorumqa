## Conclusion: the real weekly budget, and week-1 priced against it

**Measured lower bound on the 1-week quota: ~43.8M tokens** (17.9M in /
25.9M out) — the logged consumption that exhausted it, across 48 run-files.
True consumption was higher (unlogged dropped-item tokens, retries — the
invalidated AIME run alone burned up to 60x4 attempts of long thinking
traces mostly unlogged). Unknown: whether qwen3.8-preview calls carry a
premium multiplier against the quota.

This CORRECTS the plan-review fear that "one week holds ~3 pilots": the week
that exhausted the quota actually held ~48 logged run-files (~a dozen full
pilots plus many baselines/controls). The binding lesson stands (pace by
tokens, re-check headroom), but the ceiling is ~44M+, not ~3 runs.
